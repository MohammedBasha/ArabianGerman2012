who we are ?

Growth of your business is our only business

AGEX was established in 2004 as exhibitions and conferences organizer in the Middle East. It has built an impressive portfolio of B2B events covering all major industries. AGEX's events are supported by a large network of offices which play a vital role in understanding local market conditions. Its major brands now enjoy global recognition within the industries that they serve. We integrate our events and industrial publications within the supply chains of individual markets, allowing us to understand, anticipate and the meet the changes of each industry segment.


AGEX is a multi-dimensional company dedicated to staying close to its customer's needs. It focuses in organising International exhibitions & conferences as ideal platforms for foreign and local companies doing business in the Middle East
